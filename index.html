Digital Geographies Specialty Group
Schedule: AAG 2022, Virtual

The Digital Geographies Specialty Group (DGSG) of the American Association of Geographers (AAG) invites you to join us on Thursday, April 4th for our Morning Networking Session (with complimentary tea and coffee) at 8:00 AM (Washington 6, Marriott, Exhibition Level) and on Friday, April 5th for our annual Business Meeting at 11:45 AM (8229, Park Tower Suites, Marriott, Lobby Level) and our Keynote Panel on "Digital Geographies of Difference" at 5:00 PM (Roosevelt 1, Marriott, Exhibition Level), featuring panelists Melissa Gilbert, Brian Jordan Jefferson, Agnieszka Leszczynski, and Jack Gieseking, moderated by Emma Slager.

For those arriving in the D.C. area early, there will be a public keynote by Sarah Elwood at the Doing Critical GIS Workshop (co-sponsored by the DGSG) entitled "Borrowing on the Authority of the Future: Doing Critical GIS Otherwise" on Monday, April 1st at 6:30 PM, at Red Emma's Cooperative Bookstore and Coffeehouse in Baltimore.

Thank you to Nick Lally for assembling the following list of sessions related to digital geographies (DGSG-sponsored sessions are marked with an asterisk *). Please send any schedule corrections to willbpayne@berkeley.edu. If you are interested in getting involved with the DGSG, we are currently seeking nominations for a number of officer positions for next year, with nominations due by Tuesday, April 9th.
